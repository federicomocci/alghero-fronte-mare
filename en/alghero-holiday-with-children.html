Alghero is one of the most suitable destinations for a holiday with children in Sardinia. The old town is pedestrian, the beaches have shallow sandy bottoms, and the airport is just 10 minutes away.

Best beaches for children near the apartment

Lido Beach (below the apartment)

Fine sand, shallow water for dozens of meters, lifeguards in season. Perfect for little ones. Sun beds and umbrellas available for rent.

Maria Pia Beach (3 km)

Pine forest behind for natural shade, white sand, crystal clear water. Ideal for family picnics.

Le Bombarde (8 km)

One of the most beautiful beaches in the area. Turquoise water, sandy bottom. Convenient parking.

Mugoni (18 km)

Protected bay, calm water like a pool. Perfect even for the youngest children.

What to do in Alghero with children

Neptune's Caves

An adventure that pleases young and old. Reachable by boat (20 minutes from Alghero) or by the Escala del Cabirol (654 steps ‚Äî only for the most adventurous).

Alghero Aquarium

Small but interesting, perfect for an afternoon. Turtles, Mediterranean fish, touch tanks.

Walk on the ramparts

The old town ramparts are perfect for a sunset walk. Ice cream in hand, view of the sea.

Trip to Capo Caccia

By car, to see the most beautiful views of the area. Stop at the viewpoint for spectacular photos.
