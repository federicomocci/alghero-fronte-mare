üí° Tip Go early morning or late afternoon to avoid heat. Bring water, wear proper shoes.

Alghero's centro storico is Catalan in origin ‚Äî you'll hear it in the language, see it in the architecture. Wander the narrow streets, walk the sea walls at sunset, visit the cathedral.

Museum 1 hour

Alghero is known as the Coral Riviera. This small museum explains the history of coral fishing and craftsmanship. Interesting even if you're not buying jewelry.
